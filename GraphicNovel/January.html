January

To January, I thank you for the new beginnings,
the optimistic reawakening..
Even if so sweet,
I sense my worry unwavering.
The excitement of uncertainty,
the opportunity of a brand new.
Although you're filled with wonder,
fear of the unknown accompanies you.
Hope, elation and expectations each to their own
Mine beginning with passion and adventure
But reality always finds its way home,
Entering the door into the new year in gesture.
Success, joy, growth and prosperity,
No pressure.
