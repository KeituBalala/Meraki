September

It's times like this when life seems harsh
Tempted to turn heart into stone
Consistent peace is all that I ask
But it seems my request float in the unknown
So many goodbyes, without new hellos
Is this the side effect of being grown?
Watching all that you've known buried deep
Bleep bleep...
Another alarm as life continues a new week
No time to mourn or dwell in sadness
Society awaits contribution to its madness
So whatever you do, avoid excuses
Show up and leave your loved ones all the exclusives
Practicing my "I'm okay, I swear I can do this"
I promise one day it won't be illusive
