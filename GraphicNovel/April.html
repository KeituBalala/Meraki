April

What is to come from this isolation?
So are many of my questions
Conspiracy theories on overdrive
Curiosity meets anxiety
Hiding the truth of life for affirmation
How easy it is to loose site of our aspirations
Waiting for the day when we can come alive
How far gone are we from the concept of a society?
Encouraged and aspiring for growth
Introspection is the key to it all
Unlock creativity and consciousness
Understanding the purpose of this test
To be together again we must take an oath
To give every effort no matter how small
Working hard to regain the joy of confidence
Until our anxious minds can rest.
