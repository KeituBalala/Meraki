July

Chaos and beauty intertwined
The snow of winter, though numbing, seems divine
Whatever life tried to teach me I guess I missed it
Trying to be strong, but mistakenly distant
Old things die as I'm made a new
Farewell fear, I hope you take confusion with you
Once lost in thought, now basking in rest
This time I won't forget how I have been blessed
I rather bloom or aspire to grow wings
Remembering, perception is everything
I choose to feel planted and not buried
Living a life filled with joy and merry
