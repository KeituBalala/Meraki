May

What a perfect time..
To find what sets my soul alive
Understanding that purpose projects passion
I set my limits beyond the sky
Like flowers attracted to the sun
I lean towards brighter days
So I choose to do in these moments
What I will be grateful for in future Mays
Faith as a habit, Prayer as a reflex
No longer moved by what was once threatening
In time of fear, I dare show courage
Dear life, prepare yourself for my reawakening
