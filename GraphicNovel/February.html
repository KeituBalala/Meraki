February

Love and aging, thats what it should bring
Instead, this time, the load settled in
Fustration, Inadequacy, where do I begin
To summon the strength that lays deep within
Colours of treachery, shades of hopelessness
A fool to belive it could bare nobleness
No need to ponder or wait for things to be right
There is a blessing of authority over might
Independence granted to those who dare to try
Assertiveness in all the chances one takes
Now, reaching the end of its time
Peace with the bed that has been made.
