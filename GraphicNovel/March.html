March

The distance traveled could not measure up
For the trouble ahead ceases all, rather abrupt
Fatigue seemed sweeter once reminisced
For once a upon an era, life could coexist
Oh, what beauty, a time like this
Seclusion grows like one into two
Many, in ignorance, destined to bloom
Seeking answers and filled with query
Lay to rest all which was once sane and steady
How could life itself seem so deadly
Seeds of doubt sown in secret
Overwhelmed 'til burn out seemed so frequent
Rest in abundance and ubiquity
Obscuring the lack of humility
Could hope be lost for our humanity?
