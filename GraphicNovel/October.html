October

This one is for You
The One that i pray to
Messiah and Christ
He who paid the price
i've been taught of Your greatness
Your love and Your patience
How You fight and protect all You've created
Even those You deem faithless
In this time of loss and sorrow
i'm determined to keep my faith
i trust in You for my tomorrow
For i know Your will always prevails
i dedicate this month to focus only on You
And pray for humanity too
i thank You for everything You pulled me through
And ask for the world's needed breakthrough
Many are burdened and many are scared
For the time we are in is far from ideal
But, i know what we suffer now is nothing compared
To the glory that You will reveal
So i trust You my Lord, as my obedience remains
Waiting for You to remember us again
